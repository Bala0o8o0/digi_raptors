FAQ

For any inquiries or collaborations, feel free to reach out to us via Social's. We're always happy to connect with fellow NFT enthusiasts!

What are DigiRaptors?

DigiRaptors are a unique collection of NFTs featuring digital representations of Dino Goblins. Each NFT is a one-of-a-kind, digital artwork stored on the blockchain.

How can I purchase DigiRaptors NFTs?

To acquire DigiRaptors NFTs, you'll need to visit a supported NFT marketplace on the Matic network, such as OpenSea. Connect your wallet, browse the collection, and place bids or make purchases using cryptocurrency.

Are DigiRaptors eco-friendly?

Yes, DigiRaptors are eco-friendly! They are hosted on the Matic network, which uses a Proof of Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism, reducing the environmental impact compared to Proof of Work (PoW) blockchains.

Are there any benefits to holding DigiRaptors NFTs?

Yes, holding DigiRaptors NFTs can offer exclusive access to community events, early releases, and Staking in future

Can I resell my DigiRaptors NFTs?

Absolutely! You have full ownership rights and can sell your DigiRaptors NFTs on supported NFT marketplaces to other collectors.

Is there a limited supply of DigiRaptors NFTs?

Yes, each DigiRaptor NFT is part of a limited collection. Once they're sold out, no more will be created, making them even more exclusive.

What happens if I lose my DigiRaptors NFT?

Ensure you keep your NFTs secure in your wallet. If you lose access or transfer them to another address by mistake, recovery may be challenging. Always exercise caution.

How do I stay updated on DigiRaptors news and events?

Stay informed by following us on Twitter, Instagram, and joining our Discord community. We regularly share updates, news, and exciting events there.
